Our Story

In 2003, Life Formula Synergy, Inc. (LFSI) emerged. But before the company's birth, LFSI President and CEO Juvy De Ungria had a perilous pregnancy. While she was with child, she suffered from a stroke, which endangered her and her baby. In a matter of life and death, she, and her husband, LFSI's Vice President Erwin De Ungria searched for an alternative and natural way to aid her healing and recovery. Ms. Juvy and her baby were saved by Ozonized Waters from overseas - Thus, the beginning of the passion and journey to bring this natural innovation to the Philippines and make it affordable for every Filipino.

Taking off at the right place and time, with the best intentions will inevitably bring about growth and progress. Life Formula Synergy, Inc. has proven this to be certain, as the company stays true to its name and fuses together various elements for success in the perfect moment and with a heart.
